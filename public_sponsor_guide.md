## Quick Method (5 minutes):

**Step 1: Find Someone to Sponsor**
1. Go to https://github.com/sponsors/explore
2. Browse developers and projects
3. Or sponsor someone whose project you use

**Step 2: Choose a Tier**
1. Click "Sponsor" button on their profile
2. Select a tier (usually $1-5/month minimum)
3. Choose "Public" sponsorship (not private!)

**Step 3: Complete Payment**
1. Add payment method
2. Confirm sponsorship
3. Make sure it's set to **Public** (this is important!)
